Representative Work1: Counterfactual VQA: A Cause-Effect Look at Language Bias
Motivation: 1) VQA models may rely on spurious linguistic correlations rather than multi-modal reasoning. 2) It will fail to generalize well if they simply memorize the strong language priors in the training data. 3) the goal is to mitigate language bias in VQA

=== s63 ===
Representative Work1: Counterfactual VQA: A Cause-Effect Look at Language Bias
SCM and Method: capture the language bias as the direct causal effect of questions on answers and reduce the language bias by subtracting the direct language effect from the total causal effect

=== s74 ===
Representative Work5: Counterfactual inference for text classification debiasing
Motivation: Today’s text classifiers inevitably suffer from unintended dataset biases, especially the document-level label bias and word-level keyword bias, which may hurt models’ generalization.

=== s75 ===
Representative Work5: Counterfactual inference for text classification debiasing
SCM and Method: In inference, given a factual input document, CORSAIR imagines its two counterfactual counter-parts to distill and mitigate the two biases captured by the poisonous model.
Similar to representative work 1

=== s76 ===
Representative Work6: DoCoGen: Domain Counterfactual Generation for Low Resource Domain Adaptation
Motivation: Natural language processing (NLP) algorithms have become very successful, but they still struggle when applied to out-of-distribution examples.

=== s77 ===
Representative Work6: DoCoGen: Domain Counterfactual Generation for Low Resource Domain Adaptation
Method: Given an input text example, the algorithm generates a domain-counterfactual textual example (D-CON) – that is similar to the original in all aspects, including the task label, but its domain is changed to a desired one
Similar to representative work3

=== s78 ===
Representative Work7: Debiasing NLU models via Causal Intervention and Counterfactual Reasoning
Motivation: Natural Language Understanding (NLU) models are prone to relying on annotation biases of the datasets as a shortcut, which goes against the underlying mechanisms of the task of interest

=== s79 ===
Representative Work7: Debiasing NLU models via Causal Intervention and Counterfactual Reasoning
SCM and Method: 1) To remove confounder, the backdoor adjustment with causal intervention is utilized to find the true causal effect during training 2) In inference process, we formulate the bias as the direct causal effect and remove it by pursuing the indirect causal effect with counterfactual reasoning.
