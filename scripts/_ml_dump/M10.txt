=== s16 ===
A set of variables Z satisfies the frontdoor criterion relative to an ordered pair of variables (X, Y) in a DAG G if
Z intercepts all directed paths from X to Y;
there is no unblocked backdoor path from X to Z; and
all backdoor paths from Z to Y are blocked by X.
